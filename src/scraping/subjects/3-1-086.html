授業科目の概要
The aim of this course is to develop English language skills and strategies primarily for the TOEIC language exam. The skills to be covered are listening and reading, but additional focus is on speaking. By the end of the course, students will possess crucial knowledge and practical advice with the help of which their TOEIC scores can be maximized. Students will also learn to communicate and discuss topics more deeply and critically than they did in their previous language development courses.
本授業の到達目標
Overall, this course will offer crucial knowledge, practical advice, and training to help students achieve higher TOEIC scores. In this way, the course will provide guidance and support to help each student work toward individual TOEIC-related goals.
These include the following items:
1. learning how to overcome one’s own weaknesses on the exam
2. revising one’s own study goals and study plans for TOEIC
3. assessing materials to use for one’s own TOEIC-related independent learning
4. applying effective study skills and study habits for the exam
5. increasing one’s TOEIC-related vocabulary
6. applying effective test-taking strategies for each part of the exam

Upon successful completion of this course, students should be able to do the following:
1. students will be able to apply TOEIC test-taking strategies
2. learn vocabulary, grammar and expressions needed to acquire a high score on the TOEIC test
3. learn to manage time during the TOEIC test
4. be able to find and use suitable materials for independent learning
