授業科目の概要
1. Global outcomes:
Students can understand the main points of clear standard input on familiar matters regularly encountered in work,
school, leisure, etc..
By the end of the course students will achieve Level B1 on the CEFR and will be able to progress to B1+.
本授業の到達目標
This is a general English course at the level of A2 on the Common European Framework Reference (CEFR). This is
a general English course aimed at improving students’ macro language skills; speaking, writing, listening and reading.

実務教員科目
成績評価基準
Students are evaluated by the level of their class participation as well as the contents of the homework and projects.
評価の方法・総合評価割合
The course will assess all skills, with a heavier weighting towards the productive skills of writing and speaking. There will
also be a mark for student participation. The weighting is broken down below.

Participation: 40%
Homework: 20%
Project: 40%

履修上の注意事項、事前・事後学修に必要な目安時間、フィードバックの方法、学修上の助言
In the first class, teachers will provide a Code of Conduct. In order to achieve this course goal, students are required to
spend approximately 60 minutes on both preparing for and reviewing each class.

Please note that the syllabus is subject to change.
In order to pass this course, students are expected to achieve at least 60%.

【pre-task and post-task】
In order to achieve this course goal, students require about 100 hours per semester for preparation and review.

【Feedback】
Feedback will be provided in multiple ways relative to the tasks; oral feedback and written feedback. Please follow the instruction of your teacher. If you have any questions, please use Tutoring Collections (個別指導) on LMS.
