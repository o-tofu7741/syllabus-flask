授業科目の概要
This course aims to understand the concept of Discourse Studies based on various theories. The course envisages a reflective perspective on language, culture, and society. This course proposes to attain the diploma policy 3 and 8 that gain applicable knowledge for the research and analysis methods.
本授業の到達目標
The achievement is to understand Discourse Studies and utilize concepts to research theme for master thesis.
