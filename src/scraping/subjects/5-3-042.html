授業科目の概要
The course provides the course participants with an indepth view of actor-network theory. It encourages the course participants to discuss actor-network theory in philosophical, methodological, and practical dimentions. The course participants are expected to apply actor-network theory in the the data analysis of their own studies.
本授業の到達目標
The course participants are expected to be able to apply some of the concepts in their own researches. They are supposed to create their own conceptual theories on the relations between technology and human being.
