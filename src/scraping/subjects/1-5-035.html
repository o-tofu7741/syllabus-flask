Intercultural communication, interactive approach
授業科目の概要
Communication 3will be an interactive based course that aims to improve student’s awareness of culture. In this class students will be guided in how to understand various forms of culture – local, global, etc. Through the use of social media, movies/TV shows, personal experience and independent research, students will explore their own cultures, cultures from around the world and also gain skills in how to attain cultural competency.
本授業の到達目標
During the course, students will be required to watch English media, read articles and conduct research in an attempt to foster autonomous learning and exposure to foreign media.
-Understanding cultural issues and ideas through media
-Watch and analyse media (videos, TV shows, movies, pictures, etc.)
-Share ideas and questions with peers (classmates)
-Expand our knowledge and understanding
