キーワード
International Human Resource Development, Leadership, Cognitive Factors, Testing, Needs Analysis, Program Design, Training Evaluation, Monitoring,
授業科目の概要
This courses aim to develop students knowledge on needs anslyses, training methods, evaluation of training and other educational intervention, return on investment of training interventions
本授業の到達目標
Students will achieve comprehensive understanding of current human resource development issues and present and past attempts of solution for the issues that the society faces
