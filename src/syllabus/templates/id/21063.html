授業科目の概要
This course will enable students to improve their conversation and presentation skills in English. Different topics will be discussed each week, many of which will be familiar to students already. Students will usually be expected to prepare to discuss topics in advance of the class, and be expected to express their own thoughts and opinions in each class. During the course, students will also be expected to give a short presentation of some five to ten minutes in length on a topic of their own choice.
本授業の到達目標
The course aims to not only improve student’s reading skills, but writing also, with a focus on writing for different purposes. For example writing to inform, persuade, argue or story writing. Students will be asked to identify the various techniques used for each purpose, as well as write in those styles themselves.

実務教員科目
成績評価基準
This course adopts holistic assessment based on rubrics, peer-evaluation, and LMS tasks.
評価の方法・総合評価割合
Continuous Assessment - 75%
Presentation - 20%
Attendance - 5%

Please note that 75% of the grading for this course is based on continuous assessment. Students will be expected to fully participate in class discussions each week, to express their own thoughts and opinions, and to ask questions, the goal being to be able to speak and converse naturally in English. In order to do this effectively, students will usually need to prepare for lessons in advance.
The teacher will offer advice and support as necessary.
テキスト等、学修のために購入・準備が必要なもの
There is no required textbook for this course. Any materials required will be provided in class and LMS.
履修上の注意事項、事前・事後学修に必要な目安時間、フィードバックの方法、学修上の助言
Please note that the syllabus is subject to change.
In order to pass this course, students are expected to achieve at least 60%.

Pre-reading is required before each class. To gain participation marks, students need to engage in class discussions and homework assignments.

【pre-task and post-task】
In order to achieve this course goal, students require about 100 hours per semester for preparation and review.

【Feedback】
Feedback will be provided in multiple ways relative to the tasks; oral feedback and written feedback. Please follow the instruction of your teacher. If you have any questions, please use Tutoring Collections (個別指導) on LMS.
