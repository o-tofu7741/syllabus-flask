授業科目の概要
The course discusses the current trends and theories in social media communication studies. Topics cover areas such as behavior, culture, business, and social movement. It helps the course participants to update their knowledge in the field of social media research theories and methods. The course participants are expected to apply the theories and research methods in the data analysis of their own studies.
本授業の到達目標
The course participants are expected to be able to apply some of the concepts and methods in their own researches. They are expected to create their own conceptual theories on the relations between media and society.
