キーワード
doctoral dissertation, time and space, cognition, power and knowledge, Critical Discourse Analysis
授業科目の概要
This course provide guidance and super vision to the students who is in the final stage of dissertation writing. It is assumed that at this point the dissertation is tentatively completed and the course aims to examine the existing work.
本授業の到達目標
The course aims to have the student complete the doctoral dissertation and defend it

授業計画
事前事後学修内容
1
Title and abstract
The title needs to be revisited to ensure the adequacy. The student is supposed to complete the abstract of the dissertation
2
The coherence and continuity
technical terms need to be reviewed in terms of consistency and coherence
3
Grammatical aspect of wrting
Grammar needs to be checked
4
Research ethics and citation研究倫理教育
The student is supposed to check own writing to see the discussion is original and do not rely exclusively on citation
5
Theories used as a framework
The student revisits the data analysis parts to examine the theories and concepts discussed earlier are used adequately to frame the analyses.
6
definitions of the terminology
The students select important terms and ensure that definitions of each term is clearly explained
7
References to support arguments
The students ensures that discussion in the theoretical and conceptual frameworks are supported my adequate and multiple references.
8
Critical Discourse Analysis
The student ensure that Critical Discourse Analysis is an appropriate methodology for this research
9
Power, Knowledge and Cognition
The student confirm that the notion of power and knowledge is adequately explicated in the dissertation. Also the student needs to revisit cognitions used in Critical Discourse Analysis literature
10
Presentation of interview data
Thick description of the research site needs to be ensured
11
Interrelation between the analyses
The inter-relation of the analyses needs to be reviewed
12
Pedagogical application
The student re-considers application of the findings to English teaching
13
Grounded theory
The student revisit the dissertation in terms of grounding theory.
14
Future research
Possibilities of future research are envisioned
15
Presentation
Plan and rehearse the presentation for the defence

実務教員科目
成績評価基準
The course enhances understanding of doctoral research
評価の方法・総合評価割合
The student is evaluated based on the consistent writing of the dissertation (30%), reading of the related reading material (40%) and reflection on them (30%)
テキスト等、学修のために購入・準備が必要なもの
Provided on LMS
履修上の注意事項、事前・事後学修に必要な目安時間、フィードバックの方法、学修上の助言
For examination and reading , the student needs to spend at least 400 hours to work on research in total. Distribution of the total study time may depend on the personal schedule
