授業科目の概要
1. Global outcomes:
- Students will be able to deal with most situations likely to arise while traveling in an English speaking country, as well as
briefly understanding more abstract topics.
- Will be able to describe experiences, hopes, plans and ambitions and also be able to give reasons and explanations for
opinions.

2. Listening outcomes:
- Student’s will be able to understand the main points of radio or tv programmes when on topics such as current affairs or
personal interest at a faster pace than B1 level.

3. Reading outcomes:
- Students will be able to understand factual texts on subjects related to their interests.
- Will be able to identify main points of newspaper articles.
- Will be able to understand descriptions of events, feelings and wishes.

4. Speaking outcomes:
- Students will be able to use a wide range of simple language to enter into unprepared conversation on topics (interests,
family, hobbies, work, travel, current events).

5. Writing outcomes:
- Students will be able to write longer texts and be able to structure a paragraph.
- Be able to express opinion and advantages/disadvantages.
- Be able to use a wide range of simple language to enter into unprepared conversation on topics (interests, family,
hobbies, work, travel, current events).
本授業の到達目標
This is a general English course at the level of A2+/B1 on the Common European Framework Reference (CEFR). This is a general English course aimed at improving students’ macro language skills; speaking, writing, listening and reading.
Students must achieve at least 60% to pass this course.

成績評価基準
Students are evaluated by the level of their class participation as well as the contents of the homework and projects.
評価の方法・総合評価割合
The course will assess all skills, with a heavier weighting towards the productive skills of writing and speaking. There will
also be a mark for student participation. The weighting is broken down below.

1. Participation: 40%
2. Homework: 20%
3. Project: 40%
